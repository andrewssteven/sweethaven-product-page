Our Story

From a tiny oven to a neighbourhood bakery

At SweetHaven Bakery, every bite tells a story — one baked with love, patience, and a sprinkle of joy. What started as a small family kitchen filled with the aroma of freshly baked bread has grown into a cozy haven for pastry lovers who crave the taste of home.


From our golden, crusty loaves to our soft, buttery cookies, each creation is handcrafted using the finest ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. We believe that good pastries do more than satisfy hunger — they warm hearts, spark memories, and bring people closer together.


So whether you’re stopping by for your morning croissant, celebrating a special moment with a cake, or simply craving something sweet, SweetHaven is where every treat is made with care, and every visit feels like coming home.
